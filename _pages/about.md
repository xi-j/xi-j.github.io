I am a PhD candidate at [Columbia University](https://www.columbia.edu), in [Electrical Engineering](https://www.ee.columbia.edu) and [Zuckerman Institute](https://zuckermaninstitute.columbia.edu). I am a member of the [Neural Acoustic Processing Lab](https://naplab.ee.columbia.edu) led by Professor [Nima Mesgarani](https://nima.ee.columbia.edu). I build intelligent machines and agents that can listen and speak like humans. Recently, I study the perception of multimodal large language models.
